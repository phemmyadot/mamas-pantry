01 — Logo System

The Mark

Clear Space & Minimum Size

⬜

Clear Space

Always maintain a minimum clear space of the height of the "M" in "Mama's" on all sides of the logo. No text, graphics, or other logos should intrude into this space.

📏

Minimum Size

Digital: 120px wide minimum. Print: 30mm wide minimum. Below this, the sub-line "Imported & Local Provisions" should be dropped, keeping only the wordmark and emblem.

📄

File Formats

Always use SVG or PDF for digital and print. Use PNG (transparent background) only for social media profile images or where vector isn't supported. Never use JPG for the logo.

04 — Brand Voice & Tone

How We Speak

Mama's Pantry speaks like a knowledgeable neighbour, not a corporate chain. Warm, direct, a little proud of what we stock, and always honest about where things come from.

Warm & Personal

We use "we", "our", and "your family" naturally. We're not a faceless store, we're a neighbour who happens to stock the best provisions in Magodo.

"Our mum picked this one herself in Atlanta last week. It sold out in three days last time — just saying."

Honest & Transparent

We always say where a product came from. US-imported items carry their origin story. Local items carry their source region. No ambiguity, no vague "imported quality" claims.

"Locally grown in Plateau State. Packed fresh. No preservatives."

Community-Rooted

We know this neighbourhood. We reference Magodo naturally. We celebrate local celebrations and local seasons. We speak to the specific life of our community, not a generic Lagos audience.

"Happy New Year, Magodo! We're open from 8am on January 2nd. New shipment just landed."

Quietly Knowledgeable

We know food well. We share that knowledge without being preachy. A one-line tip on a shelf label. A storage note in the app. Small details that show we actually care about the product.

"Best used within 10 days of opening. Keep refrigerated. Pairs beautifully with agege bread."

Language Do's and Don'ts

Say "hand-picked in the US" not "imported goods"
Don't say "premium quality" without evidence
Use first names on staff name tags — "Hi, I'm Tola"
Don't say "valued customer" or "dear patron"
Write prices as ₦4,500 (not NGN 4500 or N4,500)
Don't write in all caps on social media posts
Reference "Mum's Picks" as a specific named category
Don't over-explain. Short sentences win.
Use Naira symbol ₦ always — this is a Lagos store
Don't write "affordable" — let the prices speak

Signage Specifications

🎨

Fascia Board

Forest Green (#1A3D26) background. Wordmark in white with the gold apostrophe. Illuminated with warm LED backlight. Minimum letter height: 200mm for readability from 10m.

🪟

Window Graphics

Frosted vinyl with Forest Green wordmark. Below: "Open 8am – 9pm Daily". One window reserved for rotating promotional graphics printed in the brand palette.

🏷️

In-Store Aisle Signs

Hanging signs on white card, Forest Green background, white DM Sans category name. Section labels: Mum's Picks, Local Provisions, Dairy & Chilled, Beverages, Household.

⭐

Mum's Picks Display

Small gold shelf talker cards: Forest Green text on Gold 100 (#FBF0CC) card stock. Label reads: "Mum's Pick — Hand-selected in the US" with a small basket icon.

🪧

Price Tags

White card, Forest Green price in DM Sans 600. Origin flag + state/country in caption style. "US Import" tags have a gold underline. Local tags have a green underline.

📖

Story Wall

At store entrance: 600x900mm printed panel on Pantry Cream stock. Forest Green headline: "How Mama's Pantry began." Body in DM Sans Regular 16pt. 3-4 sentences only.
