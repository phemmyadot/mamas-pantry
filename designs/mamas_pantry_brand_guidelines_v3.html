Usage Rules
Logo clear spaceLeave space equal to the height of the bag handle on all sides. Never crowd the mark against another element or edge.
Minimum sizeDigital: 120px wide. Print: 25mm. Below these sizes use the icon-only mark (bag without wordmark).
Approved backgroundsForest Deep (#1B4332), Warm Cream (#FEFAE0), and White (#FFFFFF) only. Never on a photographic background without a solid colour backing.
Always use the apostrophePublic-facing materials always read Mama's Pantry. Never "Mamas Pantry", "MP", or "The Pantry" in any customer-facing materials.
Gold is an accent, not a primaryHarvest Gold appears on: the "Mum's Pick" badge, logo star, price highlights, and premium callouts only. It never dominates a layout.
Admin vs customer UIThe customer website and app use Warm Cream (#FEFAE0) as the primary background. The admin web app uses neutral system backgrounds to keep the dashboard feeling clinical and efficient, not warm and retail.
